What is Mevapay, and how does it work?

Mevapay is a digital payment solution for seamless transactions, such as airtime purchases, data bundles, bill payments, and wallet funding. It works by allowing users to top-up their wallets or transact directly with linked payment methods.

Is Mevapay free to use?

Creating and maintaining a Mevapay account is free. However, minimal transaction fees may apply for specific services, which are displayed transparently before confirming any payment.

How do I verify my Mevapay account?

To verify your account, log in, navigate to the “Verification” section, and upload your identification documents (e.g., national ID, passport). Verification enhances your account limits and ensures security.

What services can I pay for on Mevapay?

On Mevapay, you can:

  • Buy airtime for all major networks.
  • Purchase mobile data bundles.
  • Pay for cable TV subscriptions (e.g., DSTV, GOTV).
  • Fund betting accounts.
  • Settle utility bills (electricity, water, etc.).

Does Mevapay support multiple currencies?

Currently, Mevapay operates in local currencies. Multi-currency support is in development and will be introduced soon.

How can I update my account information?

Log in to your account, go to “Profile Settings,” and update your personal details. For security reasons, some changes may require verification.

Can I make payments without an internet connection?

Mevapay requires an active internet connection to process transactions. Offline features are being explored for future updates.

What happens if a transaction fails?

If a transaction fails, your funds will be automatically reversed to your Mevapay wallet or original payment method. Contact support if you do not see the refund within 24 hours.

How long does it take for transactions to process?

Most transactions are processed instantly. Bank transfers and utility bill payments may take a few minutes, depending on the service provider.

How do I refer friends to Mevapay?

Share your unique referral link from the “Referral Program” section. You will earn rewards when your referrals sign up and complete their first transaction.

How secure is Mevapay?

Mevapay prioritizes security using SSL encryption, two-factor authentication (2FA), and advanced fraud detection systems to protect user information and transactions.

Can I cancel a transaction once initiated?

Transactions cannot be canceled once confirmed. However, if the transaction fails or is incorrect, funds will be reversed.

Does Mevapay offer discounts or cashback?

Yes, Mevapay periodically offers discounts, promo codes, and cashback rewards on transactions. Stay updated through our website and notifications.

How do I check my wallet balance?

Your wallet balance is visible on the dashboard. You can hide or unhide it using the “eye” icon for privacy.

Can I schedule future transactions?

Yes, you can schedule future transactions such as bill payments or wallet transfers through the “Schedule Payments” feature.

What payment methods are supported?

Mevapay supports bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile money.

What happens if I forget my password?

Click on “Forgot Password?” on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Does Mevapay have a mobile app?

Not yet, but it's coming soon! Stay tuned for updates on our mobile app launch and get ready for even more convenient transactions.

How can I track my spending?

Use the “Spending Tracker” on your dashboard to view categorized expenses and monitor your transaction history.

How do I change my notification preferences?

Log in to your account, go to “Settings,” and customize your notification preferences, including email and SMS alerts.

Is there a daily transaction limit?

Daily transaction limits depend on your verification status. Verified accounts have higher limits.

How can I stay informed about updates and new features?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or enable notifications in your Mevapay account to stay informed.
